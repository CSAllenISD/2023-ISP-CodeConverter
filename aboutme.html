About the Team

Our Mission

We have started this project under the idea that we wish for developers who want to have the most optimized algorithm to not have a language error be their halting point. Developers who have learned easy languages like Swift or Python, should optimize their program to the best ability, but these easy to learn languages have a harder time optimizing code. More optimal languages like C++ or Java, though have the capabilities to optmize your code, are very hard to comprehend and implement. Amature coders around the world should not have a programming language stop them from optimizing their algorithm. Thus, the idea behind the programming language translator, a way to convert code fro an easy to understand language, to a more optimal languge


Our project started small with only one conversion between two languages Swift and C++. In the future we wish to add more languages to the database to target more people. And these resources are all free to the developer. As a team, we do not wish on making a profit on this product, instead focusing on the well being of a developers who wstrive for an optimal sucess, in any algorithm they develop
